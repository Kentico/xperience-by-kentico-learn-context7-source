Xperience by Kentico DXP overview

Xperience by Kentico is a flexible digital experience platform that empowers business users to manage content, personalize customer experiences, and run digital marketing activities – all in one place. Whether you’re creating web content, mapping out customer journeys, or configuring user access and workflows, Xperience gives you the tools to deliver impactful customer experiences.

We recommend starting with content management and digital marketing essentials in Xperience. These core capabilities provide a solid foundation for working with more advanced features – and will help you make informed decisions that support your business goals.

Content management essentials

Content is your number one digital marketing asset. Using it effectively stands at the core of managing digital experiences.

Strong content management turns ideas into experiences. From web pages to files and multilingual content, your work as a marketer or editor directly shapes what your visitors see and how they feel. This section introduces the essential tools in Xperience by Kentico that help you create, organize, reuse, and publish content efficiently across channels. Whether you’re building a simple landing page or managing an entire multilingual site, these guides will give you the confidence and clarity to get started.

Digital marketing essentials

Xperience lets you capture and store visitor data, transform it into actionable insights, and tailor experiences across different marketing channels.

To connect meaningfully with your audience, you need to understand who your visitors are, how they behave, and what kind of content resonates with them. That’s where digital marketing in Xperience by Kentico comes in. These tools help you collect data, track how customers interact with your brand, and create personalized experiences – so you can run smarter campaigns and build stronger relationships.

The resources in this section teach you how to manage contact data, segment audiences, personalize website content, and more. These capabilities form the backbone of data-driven marketing in Xperience, giving you everything you need to stop guessing and start driving conversions.

Kentico-specific digital marketing resources

Digital marketing tutorial

  1. Manage visitor data – Learn how to uncover what your visitors are doing on your site – what content they consume and how they navigate – to gain insights that shape your customer journeys.

  2. Collect visitor data – Build forms to collect valuable visitor information and turn that data into actionable marketing insights.

  3. Segment your website audience – Use contact demographics, content preferences, and behavior to group visitors and deliver targeted content.

  4. Personalize website content – Deliver tailored content to your audience at the right moment to build stronger engagement and loyalty.

  5. Email marketing – Learn how to stay connected with your audience and promote your services through personalized email campaigns.

Digital marketing advanced topics

  1. Contact management – Explore how Xperience provides a centralized overview of all people interacting with your digital channels.

  2. Contact activities – Understand how visitor interactions – like viewing a page or submitting a form – are automatically tracked as activities tied to individual contacts.

  3. Work with forms – Learn how to create and manage online forms that are easy for visitors to fill out – plus, add custom fields, or helpful messages, and set what happens after a user submits the form.

  4. Forms – Get started with form creation and learn how forms help you gather structured, reliable data from your website visitors.

  5. Contact groups – Organize your audience into meaningful segments based on shared characteristics so you can target them more precisely in your marketing efforts.

Customer journeys

Today’s customers expect more than just relevant content – they expect meaningful experiences delivered at the right time, on the right channel. To meet these expectations, marketers need a way to design, automate, and optimize the entire customer lifecycle.

That’s where customer journeys come in.

The Customer Journeys application in Xperience helps you see how people interact with your brand, identify critical touchpoints, and create smoother paths that lead visitors from awareness to action. By guiding your audience through personalized experiences, you can drive conversions, increase loyalty, and support every stage of the customers’ journey – from first visit to long-term retention.

You’ll learn how to create and manage individual journeys, guide leads through the funnel, and deliver tailored experiences that boost engagement and loyalty. Happy customers make for happy ROI results.

Kentico-specific customer journey resources

  1. Get started with customer journeys – Learn how to analyze customer behavior to improve user engagement and increase conversions.

  2. Anatomy of customer journeys – Key components – Discover the essential building blocks of a customer journey, including journey stages, touchpoints, and customer segments.

  3. Prepare to build customer journey – Find out which internal teams should be involved and what resources you need to define and shape effective customer journeys.

  4. Customer journeys – Use the tools available in Xperience to put your knowledge into practice and build a customer journey of your own.

  5. Work with contacts – Learn how to manage contacts and contact groups and where to view each contact’s customer journey history.

  6. Activity tracking – Learn how to track visitor actions – such as page views and content consumption – to better understand how visitors move through your customer journeys.

User management essentials

Effective user and role management is key to maintaining secure, structured, and efficient collaboration within Xperience by Kentico.

Whether you’re part of a large team or managing a few contributors, user and role management ensures that the right people have access to the right tools – without compromising security. In Xperience, you can easily invite new team members, define their responsibilities, and maintain compliance with data protection regulations. Understanding how to manage users and roles will encourage a well-organized environment where everyone can work efficiently and safely. You’re protecting not only yourself but others, too.

Kentico-specific user management resources

  1. User management introduction – This short series will guide you through the essentials—logging in, creating and managing users, and defining roles for a well-organized and secure environment.

  2. *Work with roles – Learn how to use the Role management application to set up roles, assign permissions, and control access to different areas of the system.

  3. Work with users – Get comfortable with the Users application. This guide shows you how to create or edit a user and update your password.

  4. Work with users and roles – Take a deeper dive into managing users and roles. Learn how to invite users, assign them roles, reset passwords, or even set up a data protection officer role.

  5. Data protection – Explore how to manage personal data, customer consent, and data removal requests in compliance with regulations like GDPR. This guide walks you through locating, sharing, and deleting customer data using Xperience’s Data Protection feature.
